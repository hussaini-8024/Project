# GALI GANG — Full Bakwas Season 1

**Language:** Funny Urdu–Hindi mix (Hinglish street comedy)  
**Format:** 10 episodes × ~20 minutes  
**Setting:** Lalten Gali — a colourful mohalla where every plan becomes bakwas  
**Tone:** Slapstick + witty dialogue + warm neighbourhood heart  
**Tagline:** *Har gali mein ek bakwas hoti hai… yeh uski story hai!*

## Series Hook

In Lalten Gali, twelve misfits turn ordinary days into cartoon chaos.  
Bunty wants sleep. Pinky wants fame. Nano wants inventions.  
Together they accidentally save the gali… after first destroying it.

## Season Arc

Episodes 1–3: Gali Gang forms after a neighbourhood crisis.  
Episodes 4–7: Rival gali + viral fame + weird inventions escalate comedy.  
Episodes 8–10: Biggest bakwas festival, friendship test, and a sweet ending.

## Production Notes

- Target runtime per episode: **20:00** (acts ~6 / 7 / 7 minutes)
- Dialogue mostly Urdu–Hindi mix; English punchlines sparingly
- Visual gags every 20–30 seconds
- Soft moral without lecture: friendship, honesty, neighbourhood care
