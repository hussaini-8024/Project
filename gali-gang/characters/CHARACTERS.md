# Gali Gang — Character Bible

## 1. Bunty
Sleepy teenage boy in a yellow hoodie. Secretly clever, openly lazy.  
**Want:** Peace and nap time.  
**Need:** Friends who pull him into adventure.  
**Comedy:** Solves problems while half-asleep.

## 2. Pinky
Ponytail influencer wannabe with a phone glued to her hand.  
**Want:** Viral fame.  
**Need:** Real friendship over likes.  
**Comedy:** Every disaster becomes a “reel”.

## 3. Chacha Jalebi
Round, sugary, big-hearted sweet-shop uncle.  
**Want:** Feed the whole gali.  
**Need:** Accept that love ≠ only sweets.  
**Comedy:** Invents cursed snacks that cause chaos.

## 4. Inspector Bakra
Skinny inspector in oversized khaki. Brave heart, zero clues.  
**Want:** One “perfect case”.  
**Need:** Teamwork over ego.  
**Comedy:** Arrests the wrong thing every episode.

## 5. Motu Mama
Food-powered comic relief with a giant appetite.  
**Want:** Next snack.  
**Need:** Courage beyond hunger.  
**Comedy:** Follows smell of food into plot twists.

## 6. Sassy Sana
Glasses, braids, backpack of books, sharp tongue.  
**Want:** Logic to win.  
**Need:** Soften up and play.  
**Comedy:** Roasts plans with school-level precision.

## 7. Rocky Rickshaw
Cool rickshaw driver who talks to his auto like a friend.  
**Want:** Be the gali’s hero chauffeur.  
**Need:** Trust people, not only machines.  
**Comedy:** Rickshaw “replies” with horn jokes.

## 8. Nano
Tiny inventor with helmet and wild gadgets.  
**Want:** Build something legendary.  
**Need:** Safety + patience.  
**Comedy:** Inventions work… sideways.

## 9. DJ Dumroo
Beat-maker who turns buckets, plates, and pressure cookers into music.  
**Want:** Big concert in the gali.  
**Need:** Listen as much as he plays.  
**Comedy:** Music cues slapstick timing.

## 10. Begum Bubble
Floral-sari auntie, chai cup always ready, gossip network online.  
**Want:** Know everything first.  
**Need:** Use secrets to help, not hurt.  
**Comedy:** Mishears one word → gali-wide rumour.

## 11. Professor Pappu
Stained lab coat, wild hair, bubbly flasks.  
**Want:** Prove his “genius”.  
**Need:** Responsibility.  
**Comedy:** Science fair disasters weekly.

## 12. Billu Billi
Orange talking street cat, narrator and chaos commentator.  
**Want:** Fish + front-row drama.  
**Need:** Admit he cares about the gang.  
**Comedy:** Breaks fourth wall in Urdu–Hindi punchlines.
