**A-plot:** Pinky’s “24-hour live challenge” breaks the gali.

## Act 1 — Challenge Begins

- Rocky’s rickshaw gets a beauty filter and refuses to drive “looking ugly”.

- Pinky’s best moment is unrecorded kindness: helping Begum find her missing chai cup.
- When net returns, fans love the offline clip Bunty’s cousin filmed by mistake.
