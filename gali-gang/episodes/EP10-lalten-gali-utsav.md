# Episode 10 — “Lalten Gali Utsav” (Season Finale)  
**Runtime:** 20:00  
**A-plot:** Annual festival; rival gali challenges “Who is funnier?”  
**B-plot:** Gang almost splits over spotlight — then chooses togetherness.

## Cold Open
BILLU: “Season ka final. Tissue rakho… hasi ke liye.”  
Lanterns light. Music swells.

## Act 1 — Prep Wars
- Pinky wants solo stage; DJ wants sound; Nano wants fireworks; Pappu wants robots.
- Sana tries schedule; papers fly.
- Bunty assigned opening act by accident; panics; goes back to sleep-practice.
- Chacha & Motu food stall alliance vs rival “Samosa Sultan”.

## Act 2 — Almost Split
- Misunderstanding: Begum’s half-heard line “Gang khatam” (she meant “gang ka theme khatam design”).
- Friends argue; festival stalls feel empty.
- Inspector finds “clue” it was a mix-up (finally useful).
- Each character remembers a past episode favour they received.

## Act 3 — One Gali, One Show
- Combined act: sleep-rap Bunty, Pinky’s honest unfiltered hosting, Nano’s safe sparkles, DJ’s street symphony, Rocky’s rickshaw choreography.
- Rival gali joins finale dance instead of competing.
- Lanterns rise; soft moral lands without lecture: bakwas is better together.

## Final Tag (19:20–20:00)
All 12 characters freeze-frame wave.  
EVERYONE CATCHPHRASE MEDLEY (quick cuts).  
BILLU (to camera):  
“Agar hasaye hon to share karo. Agar nahi hasaye… phir bhi share karo, Begum Bubble dekh rahi hai.  
Main Billu Billi — Lalten Gali se… Full Bakwas! Season 2? Shayad. Abhi fish.”

**End credits gag:** Bunty sleeps through credits; Pinky films him; Sana facepalms; jalebi rolls past like a sun.
