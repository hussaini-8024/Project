# Episode 01 — “Jalebi Jam”  
**Runtime target:** 20:00  
**A-plot:** Chacha’s giant jalebi machine goes viral and wild.  
**B-plot:** Pinky tries to livestream; Inspector Bakra “investigates sugar crime”.

## Cold Open (0:00–1:00)
BILLU BILLI (to camera):  
“Assalamualaikum / Namaste! Main Billu Billi. Aaj Lalten Gali mein itni meethas faili… ke ants bhi dance kar rahe hain!”

Chacha unveils “Jalebi Jet 3000”. Nano claps. Bunty yawns on a charpai.

CHACHA JALEBI: “Aaj gali ko itni jalebi dunga… history books mein likha jayega!”  
BUNTY: “History baad mein… abhi blanket do.”

Machine coughs, shoots a jalebi lasso around Motu Mama.

MOTU MAMA: “Bhook emergency! Accept!”

## Act 1 (1:00–7:00) — Sweet Success
- Crowd gathers; DJ Dumroo makes beat from frying oil bubbles.
- Pinky goes live: “Hashtag JalebiJam, like karo warna jalebi nahi milegi!”
- Sana warns machine is overheating.
- Begum Bubble spreads rumour: “Chacha ne gold jalebi banayi hai!”
- Rocky’s rickshaw gets sticky wheels; he slides in circles.
- Inspector Bakra arrives: “Sugar levels illegal lag rahe hain!”

PINKY: “Wait wait — content ban raha hai!”  
SASSY SANA: “Logic suno: temperature 200 cross. Drama mat karo.”  
INSPECTOR BAKRA: “Case open! Suspect number one… jalebi!”

Machine overflows; street becomes sticky carnival.

## Act 2 (7:00–14:00) — Sticky Chaos
- Professor Pappu “fixes” it with Anti-Sticky Spray that makes everything SUPER sticky.
- People stuck to walls, cricket bat stuck to Bunty’s forehead.
- Motu tries to eat his way free; gets stuck to a samosa cart.
- Nano builds “Unstick-o-matic” from pressure cooker + fan + bubblegum.
- Billu narrates fails like a sports commentator.

PROFESSOR PAPPU: “Science hai yeh… thodi si pagal!”  
NANO: “Idea aa gaya… thoda explode ho sakta hai.”  
ROCKY: “Rickshaw bol raha hai: chal pad… phir chipak pad!”

Midpoint twist: Rival vendor from next gali, “Samosa Sultan”, hacks the recipe rumour to steal customers.

## Act 3 (14:00–19:30) — Team Fix
- Bunty, half-asleep, notices machine’s red button is actually a “pause” sticker covering the real cool switch.
- Gang works together: DJ beat syncs Nano’s gadget pulses.
- Pinky redirects livestream to teach safety instead of chaos.
- Sticky storm reverses into a soft sugar mist; kids get free mini jalebis.
- Inspector proudly “arrests” a frying ladle.

INSPECTOR BAKRA: “Case closed… matlab almost closed!”  
CHACHA: “Pehle jalebi khao, phir socho.”  
BUNTY: “Yaar… 5 minute aur so ne do.”

## Tag (19:30–20:00)
Billu licks sugar off whiskers.  
BILLU: “Moral? Too much meethas = full bakwas. Next time: aur bakwas. Bye!”

**Runtime beats:** gag every ~25s | 3 musical stings | soft moral: share, don’t show off.
