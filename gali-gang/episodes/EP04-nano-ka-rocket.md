# Episode 04 — “Nano Ka Rocket”  
**Runtime:** 20:00  
**A-plot:** Nano builds a “Rickshaw Rocket” for science week.  
**B-plot:** Rocky refuses to let his auto become a spaceship… then secretly loves it.

## Cold Open
NANO: “Idea aa gaya… thoda explode ho sakta hai.”  
ROCKY: “Rickshaw bol raha hai: mat chhed!”

## Act 1 — Build Montage
- DJ scores construction beats from hammer + pressure cooker.
- Pappu “upgrades” fuel with chili soda.
- Pinky documents “space fashion”.
- Bunty appointed Mission Control because he can sit still (sleeping counts).

## Act 2 — Accidental Launch
- Motu sneezes on launch button.
- Rickshaw-rocket hops rooftops, steals Begum’s drying sarees as parachutes.
- Inspector chases on a bicycle with training wheels.
- Sana calculates landing: “Chacha’s shop… in 40 seconds.”

## Act 3 — Soft Landing
- Gang uses jalebi syrup as landing glue strip (callback Ep1).
- Rocket lands as a parade float.
- Science week prize: “Most Creative Almost-Disaster”.
- Rocky names auto “Captain Horn”.

TAG: Billu in tiny astronaut scarf: “Space is cool. Fish is cooler.”
